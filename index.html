Our Story

Where Artistry
Meets Home

Craft Republic was born from a deep passion for transforming raw gypsum into breathtaking works of art. Based in the heart of Port Harcourt, Nigeria, we believe every home deserves the warmth and character that only handcrafted décor can bring.

From our vibrant studio, we offer immersive hands-on classes for complete beginners and seasoned artisans alike, a curated collection of finished home décor pieces, and a full range of professional-grade gypsum crafting materials.

Whether you're here to beautify your space, learn a rewarding new skill, or launch your own craft business — Craft Republic is your creative home in Port Harcourt.
